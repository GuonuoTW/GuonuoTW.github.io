# Combat Feats

For Combat Feats, you can not have its level higher than your "Special Identity" level + 1 when you're making your character. That is, if you did not take the "Special Identity" Feat, you may not choose any Combat Feats when you're making your character.

---

## Hidden Weapon Master (1~5)
Requirements: AGI 3, Athletics 2, and has profession "Light Throwing Weapons"

**Level 1: Hidden Arsenal**
When the character uses weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, the character's throwing attacks gain [High Speed] inscription equal to this feat's level.

**Level 2: Nimble Fingers**
Drawing weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class is a free action, and the character can draw as much as they want at once.

**Level 3: Storm of Blossoms**
When the character uses weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, they can perform [Short Burst]/[Long Burst]/[Full Auto]. This consumes an equal number of the same type of hidden weapons. If throwing different weapons simultaneously, use the lowest values for weapon attributes, and use the one with the lowest level for weapon special attributes and properties.

**Level 4: To Kill Two Birds with One Stone**
The character may spend an extra Standard Action, while using weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, this attack can simultaneously attack up to a number of targets equal to this feat level. You are not allowed to make multiple attacks against the same target, and the same target cannot be affected multiple times by abilities that cause area damage (such as [Full Auto]). The character may spend a point of Willpower instead to achieve this, and this ability can be included within other light weapon throwing attack actions.

**Level 5: Throw of Fate**
The character may spend an extra Swift Action, to cause all throwing attacks this turn to suffer an Unnamed Penalty up to this feat level, and gain an equal number of AS.

## Lashing (1~5)
Requirements: AGI 3, Weaponary 2 and has profession "Whip"

**Level 1: Whip Shadow**
When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to make blade attacks, they gain [High Speed] equal to this feat level.

**Level 2: Lashing Strike**
*The whip is the only human-powered cold weapon that can reach supersonic speed.*

When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to attack targets without armor defense, they gain additional DP bonus equal to this feat level.

**Level 3: Entanglement**
*Ever seen tentacles?*

When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to grapple or trip, they gain additional DP bonus equal to this feat level.

**Level 4: Flexible Whip**
*I'm not going to kill you! I'm just going to tie you up!*

After the character grapples or trips an opponent with a weapon with [Flexible Weapon] inscription, they can use a free action to make a STR contest with the opponent. For each point of victory, they can knock the opponent back 1 meter and prone. If there's an obstacle behind them, they take impact damage equal to the remaining meters, and the grapple status is released.

**Level 5: Skin and Flesh Torn**
*Thirty years of whip mastery, the crack terrifies gods and spirits.*

The character can consume a standard action and pay 1 point of Willpower, then make a blade attack using a "Flexible Weapon" against a target. This attack also causes agony points equal to the attack's success count. Cannot stack. Target makes a Fortitude save.

**Special:** You can apply this talent to long, entangling natural weapons, such as tentacles. However, your Unarmed skill must reach level 3, and you must possess the specialization for the corresponding natural weapon.

## Deflect Arrows (3)

**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 3, Perception 3

**Level 3:**
The character must have at least one hand free or possess a hand-like appendage structure with grasping capability to use this talent. When the character is hit by a ranged projectile weapon, they can make a Dexterity + Perception check, DC equal to the damage about to be inflicted. If successful, the character can deflect the weapon without taking any damage.

When using this ability, the character must be aware of the attack and cannot be caught off guard. The character cannot deflect weapons with size greater than half their own or High Speed greater than or equal to the lower of their Dexterity and Perception. The number of times this talent can be activated per round cannot exceed the character's Legendary Perception + 1.

## Lance Knight (1~3)

**Prerequisites:** Strength 3, Animal Handling skill 2, Blade skill 2

**Level 1: Charge Proficiency**
When the character performs a mounted charge, the base Defense reduction the mount suffers from charging is reduced by this talent level * 1 point. This may change the bonus cap obtainable through mounted charging.

**Level 2: Agile Charge**
Before or after the character performs a mounted charge, the character's mount can make one movement. The mount's total movement distance per round cannot exceed its base movement speed.

**Level 3: Lance Strike**
When the character is riding a creature-type mount and uses a weapon with the [Polearm] property to make a melee blade attack that causes at least 1 point of damage, the damaged target must make a Strength + Athletics (Balance) or Dexterity + Athletics (Balance) check, DC equal to the damage value received by the target. If the target fails the check, the character can choose to knock them down or knock them back a number of meters equal to the difference. If the target hits an obstacle while being knocked back, they take bludgeoning impact damage equal to the remaining meters.

## Blade Arts (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 3, Strength 3, Blade skill 2, and possess specialization "Blade"

You must use a blade weapon classified as "Curved Blade" or "Blade" with size greater than 2 and not less than half your size to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Horizontal Blade Parry**
The blade requires no conservative defense, only clean, swift, meteor-like offense.

When the character uses blade-type weapons to parry, they gain additional parry bonus equal to this talent level on defense.

**Level 2: Gold and Iron Cut Through Years**
Those who cross blades with me are not far from death. Not human, merely an instrument of slaughter. Cutting through all, this is my proper form.

As a swift action, the character can cause all melee blade attacks this round to gain [Armor Piercing] equal to the weapon's damage, but cannot exceed this talent level.

**Level 3: Unity of Man and Blade**
Man is blade, blade is man. Because blade arts are simple and unadorned, just like your qualities, you have reached this realm.

When the character makes checks related to disarming or sundering weapons, they gain proficiency bonus equal to this talent level.

**Level 4: Direct Blade Strike**
The blade requires no elaborate techniques; it needs simplicity and smooth flow.

When the character uses blade-type weapons to make melee blade attacks, the weapon damage increases by this talent level.

**Level 5: Tiger Pounces on Ground**
The blade is "the courage of all weapons," and it's said "the blade is like a fierce tiger," so the blade must be fierce and powerful. Perhaps not as graceful as a celestial sword, but absolutely domineering and mighty.

When the character uses blade-type weapons, if a unit attacks the character without causing any damage, and that unit is within the character's melee reach, the character can immediately make a melee blade attack against them as a reflexive action. This attack cannot be combined with any techniques or abilities, but passive effects function normally.

## German Zweihander (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Strength 3, Blade skill 3

You must use blade weapons classified as "Heavy Sword" to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Sweeping Cut**
A sweeping cut is the simplest powerful attack.

The character gains proficiency bonus equal to this talent level on melee blade attack checks.

**Level 2: Intimidation**
If your opponent's stance is solid, try roaring at them.

The character's Intimidation (Roar) check gains proficiency bonus equal to this talent level.

**Level 3: Cross Strike**
Heavy and powerful momentum, cutting through both person and blade in one strike.

When receiving a melee attack, the character can use a reflexive action to make a Strength + Blade (Parry) check, using this check result as your parry defense against this attack. If you do this, you don't lose your swift action next round; instead, you lose your movement action next round.

**Level 4: Spirit Harmony**
Unity of mind and body, the sword moves like thunder.

When the character makes a melee blade attack, they can use a movement action to make an Intimidation (Roar) check. The opponent contests with Will. If the target fails the contest, they suffer morale penalty equal to the difference on base Defense for 1 round. This does not reduce their defense below 0.

**Level 5: Braced Horizontal Slash**
Block the attack, then counter with a reverse slash when the enemy is wide open.

After the character receives a melee attack, they can consume a reflexive action and pay 1 point of Willpower, then make a melee blade attack against the attacker. In this attack, the character must suffer unnamed penalty equal to the opponent's attack success count, and the damage cap for this attack is reduced by half the key attribute value, but the target must also suffer unnamed penalty equal to the character's Blade skill level on base Defense (only for this attack by the character). If the target's attack caused no damage, the character doesn't suffer the attack penalty when counterattacking.

## Russian Knife Fighting (2~5)

**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 3, Blade skill 2, and possess specialization "Dagger"

You must use blade weapons classified as "Dagger" to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 2: Attack Prediction**
How should you deal with this strike?

When the character wields a blade weapon classified as "Dagger," their initiative increases by this talent level / points.

**Level 3: Advantageous Feint**
Feints can come not only from the front, but also from the side and even the back.

When the character performs a Full-Force Strike, they can choose to add half their Dexterity value as an enhancement bonus to the check instead of adding half their Strength value as an enhancement bonus to the attack check.

**Level 4: Vital Point Attack**
Lock onto weaknesses and vital points for a killing blow.

The character can ignore called shot penalties equal to this talent level. Additionally, when targets take specific location damage and make saves, they suffer penalty equal to your Blade level bonus successes.

**Level 5: Cut and Thrust**
This is how small knives should be used.

You can consume a standard action and a movement action to make two consecutive melee blade attacks against a target. The first strike doesn't cause damage; instead, add the damage that would have been caused as DP to the second strike's attack check as an enhancement bonus. These are two attacks, so the first attack causes multiple attack penalties as normal, and the first attack cannot gain attack bonus successes.

## Axe Warrior (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Strength 4, Blade skill 2, and possess specialization "Axe"

You must use blade weapons classified as "Great Axe" to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Split Mountain**
Erlang saves his mother, splitting Mount Hua with force.

The character gains proficiency bonus equal to this talent level on melee blade attack checks.

**Level 2: Crack Earth**
The might to crack earth!!

The character's weapon gains the [Mighty] property. If it already has the [Mighty] property, when attacking enemies who are parrying, it instead gains additional DP bonus equal to the weapon's size rather than half.

**Level 3: Part Seas**
What parts the sea is not the axe, but the conviction within the axe.

Now the character's weapon's [Mighty] property also takes effect on the target's base Defense. For every 1 DP the character gains from [Mighty], the target's defense is reduced in the order of parry defense, dodge defense, and base Defense. The DP bonus from the [Mighty] property can now exceed the target's parry defense value.

**Level 4: Tear Heaven**
The intimidation that tears through heaven and earth.

When the character makes melee blade attacks, the weapon damage increases by this talent level.

**Level 5: Shatter Universe**
In chaotic darkness of the universe, Pangu swings his axe to break heaven and earth.

As a swift action, the character can cause all melee blade attacks this round to suffer unnamed penalty up to this talent level, gaining an equal number of bonus successes.

## Archer (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Strength 3, Dexterity 3, Archery skill 3

You must use weapons classified as "Bow" to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Archer's Gift**
Every inch of muscle in your arms exists for drawing and shooting arrows.

When determining your Strength requirement and maximum range for using archery, your Strength is considered to gain bonus equal to this talent level over its original value.

**Level 2: Rapid Loading**
You draw and shoot arrows at a dizzying speed, like a flash of light.

Drawing arrows from a quiver or arrow case is a free action, and the character can draw the required quantity at once.

**Level 3: Arc Shot**
You shoot arrows high, using parabolic principles to kill enemies.

When the character uses a bow to make ranged attacks, the base range doubles. If the target has defense bonuses from cover, you can ignore up to this talent level * 1 point of that bonus.

**Level 4: Multishot**
You nock multiple arrows on the string at once, attacking multiple enemies.

As a standard action, when you use a weapon with the "Bow" classification to make a ranged attack, this attack can simultaneously attack up to a number of targets equal to this talent level. Cannot make multiple attacks against the same target, and the same target cannot be affected multiple times by abilities that cause area damage (such as Full Auto). If you spend 1 point of Willpower, no action is required, and it's included in other archery attack actions.

**Level 5: Split the Willow at a Hundred Paces**
Your archery can rival Lie Yukou.

As a standard action, you can use a bow to make an attack and gain proficiency bonus equal to your Archery skill level on this attack. If you spend 1 point of Willpower, no action is required, and it's included in other archery attack actions.

## Kung Fu (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 3, Unarmed skill 2

You must attack with natural weapons to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Agile Strike**
Four ounces deflect a thousand pounds.

The character gains proficiency bonus equal to this talent level on melee unarmed attack checks.

**Level 2: Iron Body**
One force defeats ten techniques.

The character's melee unarmed attacks can cause serious damage, and for every 1 bonus success on the Unarmed skill, their natural weapon damage increases by 1L.

**Level 3: Mysterious Agility**
Fighting without agile movement is as difficult as chasing a rabbit with a tractor.

The character can consume a standard action and a swift action to make a melee unarmed attack and unarmed parry. You can use Dexterity instead of Strength for the unarmed attack check.

**Level 4: Whirlwind Flurry**
In martial arts, only speed is unbeatable.

The character can consume a standard action to make two melee unarmed attacks. These two attacks can target different opponents or the same target. Both attacks suffer 5 DP unnamed penalty and are considered a single attack.

**Level 5: Stunning Fist**
Wah-cha!

The character can consume a standard action and pay 1 point of Willpower, then make a melee unarmed attack against a target. This attack also causes paralysis points equal to the attack's success count. Cannot stack. Target makes a Fortitude save. If spending 3 points of Willpower instead, no action is required, and it's included in other melee unarmed attack actions.

## Staff Arts (1~5)

**Prerequisites:** Strength 3, Dexterity 2, Blade skill 2

You must use weapons classified as "Long Staff" or "Short Staff" to use and gain all effects from this talent.

**Level 1: Divine Staff**
Great skill appears clumsy.

The character gains proficiency bonus equal to this talent level on melee blade attack checks.

**Level 2: Leg Sweep**
A fallen person poses no threat.

The character can use weapons classified as "Long Staff" or "Short Staff" to perform trips, and melee reach increases by 5 meters.

**Level 3: Temple Strike**
One blow to knock out.

The character's weapon gains the [Stun] property. Additionally, the character can ignore called shot penalty equal to this talent level * 1 point when making melee blade attack checks.

**Level 4: Divine Weapon Does Not Kill**
The staff is a divine weapon that does not kill.

When the character makes melee blade attack checks, if the target's health bar is filled with serious damage or more than half filled with malignant damage, the character can choose not to cause damage to the target, but instead cause equal stun points. Can stack. Target makes a Fortitude save.

**Level 5: Danger Zone**
One inch longer, one inch stronger.

When the character uses weapons classified as "Long Staff" or "Short Staff," all hostile targets within reach treat the area as natural difficult terrain. As long as a target is within your reach, all their checks suffer 5 DP environmental penalty. This is an environmental penalty brought by the environment.

**Special:** Long blades, spears, and halberds that evolved from staffs and possess the [Polearm] property can also use this series of talents, as determined by the ST.
