# Combat Feats

For Combat Feats, you can not have its level higher than your "Special Identity" level + 1 when you're making your character. That is, if you did not take the "Special Identity" Feat, you may not choose any Combat Feats when you're creating your character.

---

## Hidden Weapon Master (1~5)
Requirements: AGI 3, Athletics 2, and has profession "Light Throwing Weapons"

**Level 1: Hidden Arsenal**
When the character uses weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, the character's throwing attacks gain [High Speed] inscription equal to this feat's level.

**Level 2: Nimble Fingers**
Drawing weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class is a free action, and the character can draw as much as they want at once.

**Level 3: Storm of Blossoms**
When the character uses weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, they can perform [Short Burst]/[Long Burst]/[Full Auto]. This consumes an equal number of the same type of hidden weapons. If throwing different weapons simultaneously, use the lowest values for weapon attributes, and use the one with the lowest level for weapon special attributes and properties.

**Level 4: To Kill Two Birds with One Stone**
The character may spend an extra Standard Action, while using weapons within "Hidden Weapon" or "Light Throwing Weapons" class to make throwing weapon attacks, this attack can simultaneously attack up to a number of targets equal to this feat level. You are not allowed to make multiple attacks against the same target, and the same target cannot be affected multiple times by abilities that cause area damage (such as [Full Auto]). The character may spend a point of Willpower instead to achieve this, and this ability can be included within other light weapon throwing attack actions.

**Level 5: Throw of Fate**
The character may spend an extra Swift Action, to cause all throwing attacks this turn to suffer an Unnamed Penalty up to this feat level, and gain an equal number of AS.

## Lashing (1~5)
Requirements: AGI 3, Weaponary 2 and has profession "Whip"

**Level 1: Whip Shadow**
When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to make blade attacks, they gain [High Speed] equal to this feat level.

**Level 2: Lashing Strike**
*The whip is the only human-powered cold weapon that can reach supersonic speed.*

When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to attack targets without armor defense, they gain additional DP bonus equal to this feat level.

**Level 3: Entanglement**
*Ever seen tentacles?*

When the character uses weapons with [Flexible Weapon] inscription to grapple or trip, they gain additional DP bonus equal to this feat level.

**Level 4: Flexible Whip**
*I'm not going to kill you! I'm just going to tie you up!*

After the character grapples or trips an opponent with a weapon with [Flexible Weapon] inscription, they can use a free action to make a STR contest with the opponent. For each point of victory, they can knock the opponent back 1 meter and prone. If there's an obstacle behind them, they take impact damage equal to the remaining meters, and the grapple status is released.

**Level 5: Flesh Splitting**
*Specializing in the lash for thirty years, the crack of the whip startles ghosts and gods*

The character can spend a Standard Action and pay 1 point of Willpower to perform a Weaponary attack on a target using a [Flexible Weapon]. This attack will also inflict Painful Points on the target equal to the number of successes from this attack. This effect does not stack, and the target resists with a Will Saving Throwing.
Special: You may apply this Feat to long natural weapons capable of entangling, such as tentacles. However, your Brawl skill must reach Level 3, and you must possess the corresponding Profession for that natural weapon.

## Deflect Arrows (3)
Requirements: AGI 3, PCT 3

**Level 3:**
The character must have at least one free hand or possess an arm-like structure with grasping capabilities to use this. When the character is hit by a Ranged solid projectile weapon, they can perform an AGI + PCT Check, DC is the damage the opponent is about to deal. If successful, the character can deflect the weapon and take no damage.
To use this ability, the character must be able to perceive the attack and cannot be Surprised. The character cannot deflect weapons with a Size larger than half the character's own size, or weapons with a [High Speed] rating greater than or equal to the lower of your AGI and PCT. The number of times this Feat can be activated per Round cannot exceed the character's Legendary PCT + 1.

## Lancer (1~3)
Requirements: STR 3, Animal Ken 2 and Weaponary 2

**Level 1: Charge Proficiency** 
When the character performs a mounted charge, the reduction to Base Defense the mount suffers due to charging is reduced by 1 point per level of this Feat. This may alter the maximum bonus limit obtainable through a mounted charge.
**Level 2: Flexible Charge** 
Before or after the character performs a mounted charge, the character's mount may perform a movement. The total distance the mount moves per Round cannot exceed its base movement speed.
**Level 3: Spike** 
When the character controls a biological vehicle (生物類載具), if they use a weapon with the [Polearm Weapon] inscription to make a Melee Weaponary attack and deal at least 1 point of damage, the damaged target must perform a STR + Athletics (Balance) or AGI + Athletics (Balance) Check. The DC equals the damage value received by the target. If the target fails the Check, the character can choose to knock them down or knock them back a number of meters equal to the difference. If the target hits an obstacle during the knockback, they suffer DMG(B) equal to the remaining meters.
